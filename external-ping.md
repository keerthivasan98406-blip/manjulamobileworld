# External Keep-Alive Services

If you want additional reliability, you can use these free external services to ping your website:

## 1. UptimeRobot (Recommended)
- Website: https://uptimerobot.com
- Free plan: 50 monitors, 5-minute intervals
- Setup: Add your website URL: https://manjulamobilesworld-whwt.onrender.com
- Benefit: Also provides uptime monitoring and alerts

## 2. Pingdom
- Website: https://www.pingdom.com
- Free plan: 1 monitor, 1-minute intervals
- Setup: Monitor your /health endpoint

## 3. StatusCake
- Website: https://www.statuscake.com
- Free plan: 10 monitors, 5-minute intervals

## 4. Freshping
- Website: https://www.freshworks.com/website-monitoring/
- Free plan: 50 monitors, 1-minute intervals

## Setup Instructions:
1. Sign up for any of these services
2. Add your website URL: https://manjulamobilesworld-whwt.onrender.com
3. Set monitoring interval to 5-10 minutes
4. Enable email alerts for downtime

This will keep your Render service awake 24/7!
